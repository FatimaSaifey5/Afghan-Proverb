Afghan proverbs hold deep cultural wisdom, passed down through generations to teach patience, humility, and honor. Explore the richness of Afghan oral tradition.

صبر تلخ است ولی میوه‌اش شیرین است

Patience is bitter, but its fruit is sweet.

با صبر ترش، شیرین می‌شود

With patience, the sour turns sweet.
