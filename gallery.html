از کوزه همان برون تراود که در اوست

What’s in the pot comes out of it.

One's true nature reveals itself.

با یک گل بهار نمی‌شود

One flower does not bring spring.

One person alone can't bring change.

کار امروز را به فردا مسپار

Don't leave today's work for tomorrow.

Be diligent and proactive.

سنگ بزرگ نشانه نزدن است

A big stone is a sign of not hitting.

Don't aim too high if you won't follow through.
